Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
Is this website free to use?
Yes, the website is completely free to use. You can access all lessons, resources, and practice materials without any cost.
Do I need to create an account?
Currently, you don’t need an account to use the website. You can freely browse and learn as a guest. In the future, we may introduce an account system so learners can save their progress.
How can I build my English vocabulary?
You can build your English vocabulary by practicing new words every day, reading books and articles to learn words in context, and reviewing them regularly. Try to use new words in writing and speaking so they become part of your active vocabulary. Quizzes and practice exercises on the website can also help you improve.
Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
At the moment, we do not provide certificates. However, we may introduce certificates in future updates.
